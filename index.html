Mission

Empowering businesses and individuals to create, exchange and capture value in the decentralized creative economy

Products, platforms, protocols. Where will your business capture value?

Blockchain technology has opened the door for inclusive business models. Individuals can now create, own and exchange valuable, funtional assets (tokens), essential to interactions with brands and services. The feature is digital scarcity, the benefit is disintermediation of value creation and exchange. Product is what you sell, platform is where you broadcast and protcol is where customers participate. The future of perceived value is capturing the network between individual actions and new business models. How will you capture value?

Soloblock is Solutions

A full service business, smart contract and decentralized application consultancy; Soloblock prides itself on being closely involved with the blockchain community. The future of work, value creation and customer engagement is not developed by applying old business models to new technology. The future is being written and experimented on by software developers today. Token engineering is an emerging field designing effective cryptoassets to jump start networks, incentivize user generated content, enhance loyalty programs and enrich data. Soloblock participates with several working groups on token engineering methodologies, cryptoasset valuation models and standards. We're here to design effective systems that grow businesses and empower individuals.
